Book: Think Fast and Slow. Daniel Kahneman.

Oct 1, 2020

This book is a must-read for everyone! If you have never read about behavioral economics, Think Fast and Slow is a great place to start. How special someone's work has to be to receive a Nobel Prize in Economic Sciences without being an economics professional? That's how extraordinary this book is!

I recommend Think Fast and Slow because it is a book that changed the way I think. After reading, I keep finding circumstances in which I don't act as the economically rational human being I am supposed to be. I now understand why I act in such a fashion and might make better decisions.

Who am I?

My name is Daniel. I'm a Costa Rican living in Toronto. I moved to Canada after four years of studying and working in the United States.

Education

I'm mainly dedicated to finance and economics, which was the concentration of my Master's in Public Administration from Cornell University.

I'm a Chartered Financial Analyst Level II candidate from the CFA Institute.

Aspirations

I'm a self-taught programmer looking to thrive at the intersection between Finance, Computer Science, Statistics, and Business Analytics.

I have experience with Python, SQL, Tableau, HTML, and CSS.

Family and Friends

Interests
